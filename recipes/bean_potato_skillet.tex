\begin{recipe}{\texorpdfstring{$\star$}{str}Bean and Potato Skillet}{4 servings - 432 calories}{}

\freeform From Canadian Living.

\Ing{4\nicefrac{1}{2} olive oil , divided}
\Ing{1 onion, thinly sliced}
\Ing{1 large yellow-fleshed potato , scrubbed and diced}
\Ing{Generous amount of salt, to taste}
\Ing{1 tbsp smoked paprika}
\Ing{4 cups thinly sliced cabbage}
\Ing{1 tbsp white wine vinegar}
\Ing{1 540 ml can navy beans , drained and rinsed}
\Ing{4 eggs}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{2} cup Balkan-style plain yogurt}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{4} cup chopped fresh dill}
\Ing{lemon wedges}

In large nonstick skillet, heat 4 tbsp of the oil over medium heat; cook onion, potato, all but a pinch of the salt and paprika, stirring occasionally, until vegetables begin to brown, about 7 minutes.\\

Stir in cabbage, vinegar and \nicefrac{1}{3} cup water; cover and cook until cabbage is wilted and softened, about 5 minutes. Remove lid and cook until cabbage begins to brown and liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes. Stir in beans; cook until potatoes and cabbage are tender, about 4 minutes.\\

Meanwhile, in separate nonstick skillet, heat remaining \nicefrac{1}{2} tbsp oil over medium heat. Cook eggs, sprinkling with remaining pinch of salt, until whites are set but yolks are still runny, about 3 minutes. Combine yogurt and dill, reserving some dill for garnish.\\

Divide potato mixture among 4 plates; top each with yogurt mixture, 1 fried egg and dill. Serve with lemon wedges.

\end{recipe}
