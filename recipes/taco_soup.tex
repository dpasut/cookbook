\begin{recipe}{\texorpdfstring{$\star$}{str}Taco Soup}{4 servings - 352 calories}{}

\freeform From 101 Cookbooks.

\Ing{2 tbsp olive oil}
\Ing{1 large onion chopped}
\Ing{3 cloves garlic chopped}
\Ing{3 tablespoons taco seasoning (p. \pageref{components:taco_seasoning})}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{2} cup quinoa rinsed}
\Ing{4 cups water}
\Ing{2 14- ounce cans pinto beans rinsed OR 3\nicefrac{1}{2} cups cooked pinto beans}
\Ing{1\nicefrac{1}{2} cups corn}
\Ing{1 14- ounce can crushed tomatoes}
\Ing{1 cup favorite red salsa\\}
\Ing{\textbf{Serve with:}}
\Ing{toasted pepitas or almonds, cilantro, avocado, salted yogurt, lime wedges}

In a pot, heat the olive oil. When hot, saut√© the onion and garlic until softened, 5-7 minutes. Stir in the taco seasoning. Add the quinoa, water, salt, beans, corn, tomatoes, and salsa.\\

Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer. Place the lid on and simmer until quinoa is cooked, about 10 minutes. Serve.

\end{recipe}
