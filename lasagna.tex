\begin{recipe}{Barry's Killer Lasagna}{6 servings - 710 calories}{}

\freeform From {\normalfont The No Fad Good Food 5\$ a Week Cookbook} by Caroline Ackerman (p. 92).

\Ing{2 tbs. oil}
\Ing{1 cup diced onions}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{2} cup diced carrots}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{2} diced celery}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{4} tsp. garlic powder\\}
\Ing{1 \nicefrac{1}{2} tsp. oregano}
\Ing{\nicefrac{1}{4} tsp. celery seed}
\Ing{1 tsp. thyme}
\Ing{1 \nicefrac{1}{2} tsp. ground rosemary}
\Ing{1 \nicefrac{1}{2} tsp. basil}
\Ing{1 tsp. chili powder\\}
\Ing{2 cups tomato paste}
\Ing{1 tbs. honey}
\Ing{2 cups water\\}
\Ing{1 package (375 g) oven ready lasagna, or fresh lasagna}
\Ing{8 oz cheddar}
\Ing{8 oz mozzarella}
\Ing{1 \nicefrac{1}{2} lbs dry cottage cheese}

\textbf{Sauce}

Heat oil in large skillet. Add onions and cook until translucent. Add carrots, celery, and garlic powder and cook for 5 minutes. Mix in all the other herbs spices and saut√© for 1 minute, then stir in the tomato paste, honey, and 2 cups water.\\

\textbf{Lasagna}

Cover bottom of pan sparsely with sauce, then a layer lasagna, cottage cheese,
cheddar, sauce, lasagna, cottage cheese, mozzarella and a little sauce.\\

Bake at 375$\degree$F for 50 minutes or until lasagna is bubbling. Let cool for 10 minutes and serve with a crisp salad.

\end{recipe}
